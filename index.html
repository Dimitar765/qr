About Us

The company Material Testing and Consulting LLC was founded in 2021. The main activity of IMK LLC is laboratory testing in the field of construction and industry, as well as consulting services in the industrial sector. With successful operations in its areas and the professional staff the company possesses, in 2022, a potential partner from Switzerland showed interest in the company. In 2023, IMK LLC transitioned from a domestic company to an international company by acquiring a partner from Switzerland and became IMK DOO. In the same year, the company received financial support from the SEKO startup fund, a fund that recognized the quality and vision of the two young entrepreneurs. The laboratory is fully equipped to meet any challenge in the field of material quality testing in the construction industry, with the latest instruments for field and laboratory testing. Material testing is carried out according to the latest EN and MKS standards.
